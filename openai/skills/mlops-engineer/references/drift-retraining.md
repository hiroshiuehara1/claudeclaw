# Drift and Retraining

Use this guide to control quality degradation over time.

Define thresholds for:
- Data distribution shift
- Feature distribution shift
- Prediction quality degradation

## Retraining Policy

Define:
- Trigger criteria
- Retraining cadence and urgency levels
- Validation gates before re-promotion

## Rollback and Recovery

Define:
- Conditions to revert model version
- Post-rollback validation checks
- Corrective action ownership
